Rational Imbalance

I am stuck to this rat race
you call civilisation,No need to be cautioned,
but getting to the realization that,this acculturation
is actually madness.

When television stations become
imitations of pornographic organisation
Instilling the sensation of fornication
in our youth,leading to illegitimate
coition. This nation needs redemption.

When human beings fail to realise
that,we are just creation from clay
not made for mere play
So all this monkey business is a crime
A genration led by sensations

But there is time for correction,
a change in direction.
An effort to rationalise our perceptions
No need for assumptons

We need actions,solutions that
will lead this nation into its perfection

Sunrise Waits For Mother

Mother is my matter wholly
She is an undaunted titan
floating without sinking
annilating adverse calamities
an edifying soul,void of quietness

Rising adversity craves security
She struggles with the toilof
a stranger.Rare benevolence paid
with ungrateful gestures
A slave who gave birth to her master

Emulatresses fail in their endeavour
to mother.Long life in her right hand,
and in her left hand,are wealth and honor
Benearth her feet rests paradise
Sunrise waits for mother.

Past Hero of The Future

A herald endowed with strength and stamina
The glory of Hera,an ever-lasting star
Living a life filled with adventure
struggling with love,lust and departure
Herakles,son of zeus

Living to embrace challenges
in succesion,seeking redemption.
>What made him a boy,made us men
Once a demi-god,now a legend.
